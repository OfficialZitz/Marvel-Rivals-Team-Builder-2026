What Is Role Balance in Marvel Rivals?

Marvel Rivals uses three roles: Vanguards (tanks and frontline anchors), Duelists (damage dealers and assassins), and Strategists (healers and support). The standard meta balance is 2-2-2 — two of each role — though aggressive dive comps often run 1-3-2 and bunker comps can run 2-1-3.

Running zero Strategists is the most common mistake in ranked. Without a healer your team has no sustain and will lose attrition fights. The team builder flags this automatically and recommends the right Strategist for your existing picks.
